Figgie

A 40-card market game. One suit secretly has 12 cards (the goal suit), its same-colour partner has only 8, and the other colour's two suits have 10 each. Everyone is dealt 10 cards and trades for 75 seconds — the only clues you get are the cards already in your own hand. Whoever ends up holding the most goal-suit cards splits a bonus on top of a fixed payout per card.
